Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Summary: We use essential cookies only. No tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies are used. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your experience.

Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and IndexedDB, which we may also use for similar purposes.

2. How We Use Cookies

TermiVoxed uses cookies for the following purposes:

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the application to function properly:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
auth_token Maintains your login session Session / 7 days
csrf_token Protects against cross-site request forgery Session
consent_given Remembers your privacy consent choice 1 year

2.2 Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your settings and preferences:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
theme Remembers your preferred theme (dark/light) 1 year
language Remembers your language preference 1 year
tts_consent Remembers your TTS data consent 1 year

2.3 Local Storage

We use browser local storage for:

  • Caching application state for faster loading
  • Storing project drafts temporarily
  • Remembering recently used voices and settings

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set their own cookies:

3.1 Firebase (Google)

Used for authentication. Firebase may set cookies to maintain your login session. Firebase Privacy Policy

3.2 Stripe

Used for payment processing. Stripe sets cookies for fraud prevention. Stripe Privacy Policy

3.3 Razorpay

Used for payment processing in India. Razorpay may set cookies for security. Razorpay Privacy Policy

4. Cookies We Do NOT Use

TermiVoxed does NOT use:

  • Advertising cookies: We don't serve ads or track you for advertising
  • Analytics cookies: We don't use Google Analytics or similar tracking
  • Social media cookies: We don't embed social media tracking widgets
  • Cross-site tracking: We don't track your activity across other websites

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using TermiVoxed properly. Authentication and session management require cookies.

6. Desktop Application

The TermiVoxed desktop application uses local storage for preferences and session data. This data is stored on your device and is not shared with our servers except for authentication tokens when connecting to cloud services.

7. Your Consent

By using TermiVoxed, you consent to our use of essential cookies as described in this policy. For the desktop application, data is stored locally with your implicit consent when you install and use the application.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
